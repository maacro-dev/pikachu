No visit to Iloilo is complete without a steaming bowl of La Paz Batchoy. The dish originated in the 1930s in the La Paz public market and has since become the definitive symbol of Ilonggo food culture. The best versions are found at small, no-frills stalls that have been serving the same recipe for generations.
