Iloilo City stands as a testament to the Philippines' colonial past and vibrant present. Walking through the heritage district of Calle Real, you'll find beautifully restored Spanish-era buildings now housing boutique cafes, galleries, and shops. The city's coastline along the Iloilo Strait offers stunning sunsets, while the Esplanade has become one of the most beloved public spaces in the Visayas.
