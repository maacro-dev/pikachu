Dinagyang is held on the fourth Sunday of January and is consistently ranked among the best festivals in Asia. Competing tribes paint their bodies, don elaborate costumes, and perform intricate choreographies to the deafening beat of drums. The festival merges deep Catholic devotion with pre-colonial indigenous traditions.
